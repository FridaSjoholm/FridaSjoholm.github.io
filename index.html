What I Do

I build beautiful and functional web apps using a mixture of Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, SQL, HTML and CSS. When deploying the app for iOS I typically turn to Swift. Creativity has always been a central part of my life and has taken many different forms, from my line of necklaces that I sold to friends and strangers on Instagram, to sewing clothes, crafting furniture and taking photos. When I moved to San Francisco my creativity moved even more to computers. Knowledge is key to creative freedom, so after many curious hours on the internet and in books, I turned to Dev Bootcamp. This intensive program taught me many great things: teamwork, new languages, how to leverage the ocean of web APIs and how to adopt new frameworks and languages quickly and effectively. All necessary tools to build apps from the ground and all the way up.

Who I Am

I spent the first 22 years of my Life in Sweden. After three out of four and a half years in Swedish law school my husband got an offer to work in the Bay Area and we decided to take the opportunity. We both fell in love with the city of San Francisco at our very first visit and for me this was a natural opportunity to try something new. Before lawschool (and partially during) I ran my own company creating webpages for small business, so when I now had more time I naturally started diving deeper in how to make better and better webapps and programs. It's a new and exciting chapter that I just can't wait to dive deeper into.
