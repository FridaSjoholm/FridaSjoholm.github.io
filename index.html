What I Do

I can build awesome websites and iOS Apps using languages like Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, Swift, SQL, HTML and CSS. I have always liked to create stuff wich has taken the form of my line of necklaces that I sold to friends and strangers on Instagram, clothes, photographing, furnitures and a ton of other projects. After moving to San Francisco I finally made the move to computers. o me, knowledge is the key to being creative with new instruments and after many curious hours on the internet and in books I took the step to study at Dev Bootcamp. In the intensive program I have learned about many new languages, but also about how to work in a team and learning new programming languages quickly and effectively.
